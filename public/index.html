About Bidaya

Where innovation meets opportunity

Our Mission

Bidaya is an independent, student-led nonprofit initiative operating under the sponsorship of a U.S.-registered 501(c)(3) public charity for support. We build a space where young people can grow through hackathons, collaborative projects, and creative opportunities while developing leadership, teamwork, and practical skills.

Global Significance

Our events help students earn certificates and rewards, strengthen their portfolios, access volunteering opportunities, and gain meaningful experience that can support university applications and recommendation letters. We focus on giving students real opportunities to stand out and grow with confidence.

Student Rewards & Opportunities

Students can win a wide range of rewards through our online and in-person hackathons and workshops, including free domains, Minecraft, food items, and other exciting prizes. Whether they are exploring CAD, web design, or other creative challenges, Bidaya is built to keep participation rewarding and help students stay motivated.
