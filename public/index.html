About Bidaya

Where innovation meets opportunity

Our Mission

Bidaya is an international student-led initiative built to empower young people through debates, hackathons, collaborative projects, and creative opportunities. We provide students with resources, mentorship, prizes, and volunteering hours while helping them build real-world leadership and teamwork skills.

Global Significance

Bidaya hosts hackathons, debates, workshops and more to empower students with such opportunities and raise their voices, so that they are heard. Bidaya will assist ambitious students pave their future

Founded by Students

Led by an exceptional team of student leaders, Bidaya represents the next generation of tech leaders building communities that matter. We're not just participants in the future - we're creating it.
