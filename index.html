About Bidaya

Where innovation meets opportunity

Our Mission

Bidaya is an international student-led initiative built to empower young people through debates, hackathons, collaborative projects, and creative opportunities. We provide students with resources, mentorship, prizes, and volunteering hours while helping them build real-world leadership and teamwork skills.

Global Impact

With structured departments and strong backing, Bidaya is designed for scale. We're organizing official debates, hackathons, and competitions to give students platforms to showcase their abilities on a global stage, expecting to reach 2,000 students in just one month.

Founded by Students

Led by Founder Huzaifa and a passionate team of student innovators, Bidaya represents the next generation of tech leaders building communities that matter. We're not just participants in the future - we're creating it.
